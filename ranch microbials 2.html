Immunity-T

Immunity-T is a bio-based agricultural product containing beneficial microbes to enhance crop growth and health. It promotes root development, suppresses pathogens, boosts plant immunity, and increases yields by up to 10-15%. Suitable for all crops, it can be applied via seed, soil, root treatments, or irrigation. Store in a cool place and use before expiry.

Liquid Consortia Nemato Wrack – Horticulture

Liquid Consortia Nemato Wrack is a bio-product for horticultural crops, containing rhizobacteria that boost root growth, nutrient uptake, and plant resistance while suppressing nematodes. It improves productivity by 10-15% and can be applied via seed, soil, root treatments, or irrigation. Store in a cool place and use before expiry.

Liquid Consortia Nemato Wrack – Vegetable

Liquid Consortia Nemato Wrack for vegetables contains beneficial rhizobacteria that enhance root growth, nutrient uptake, and plant immunity while suppressing nematodes. It boosts crop yields by 10-15% and can be applied via seed, soil, or root treatments, or irrigation. Store in a cool, shaded place and use before expiry.

Nutra Booster

Nutra Booster is a biofertilizer with nitrogen-fixing, phosphate- and potash-solubilizing bacteria, enhancing soil fertility and crop productivity. It promotes root growth, nutrient availability, plant immunity, and yields by 10-15%. Suitable for all crops, it’s used in seed, soil, and root treatments and drip irrigation. Store in a cool place and use before expiry.
